# UnCloset

A product concept reimagining how people access fashion through a clothing rental platform focused on affordability, convenience, and sustainability.

UnCloset combines user research, product strategy, UI/UX design, and prototyping to address wardrobe challenges faced by students and young professionals.

---

## Overview

UnCloset began with a simple question:

**Why do people keep buying clothes they wear only a handful of times?**

To explore this, our team conducted user research, validated assumptions, designed a digital solution, and built an interactive prototype. The project evolved into a product concept that was later adopted as the foundation for an MBA final-year project.

---

## Problem Statement

Many students and young professionals face common wardrobe challenges:

* Limited budgets for new clothing
* Lack of wardrobe variety
* Storage constraints
* Clothes being worn only a few times before being forgotten
* Increasing concerns around fashion waste

While online shopping has made fashion more accessible, it has also contributed to overconsumption and underutilization of clothing.

UnCloset aims to make fashion more accessible while encouraging a more sustainable way of consuming clothing.

---

## Solution

UnCloset is a subscription-based clothing rental platform where users can discover, rent, exchange, and return outfits through a simple digital experience.

The platform focuses on creating a seamless wardrobe experience rather than simply renting clothes.

## User Research

To validate the problem and understand user behavior, we conducted research with **200+ participants**.

The research focused on:

* Shopping habits
* Outfit repetition
* Budget constraints
* Clothing ownership
* Sustainability awareness
* Willingness to rent clothing

The insights directly influenced the product's feature set and subscription model.
